Installation and licensing

The CPLEX connector for MATLAB® does not require a separate license as a feature of IBM® ILOG CPLEX Optimization Studio. If you are licensed to use CPLEX, the same license supports the CPLEX connector for MATLAB. If you have not yet set up CPLEX, first follow those procedures for installation and licensing before you begin using the CPLEX connector for MATLAB.
Instructions for installation and licensing (if applicable) are available at the IBM Support Portal under the topic Choose your task: Installation.
