How to Use This Documentation

  • Click on the ILOG logo to access the ILOG web site.

  • Open another manual by selecting its name from the combo box.

  • Click on the pictogram featuring a page with a green arrow pointing to the number one to go to the first page of the current manual.

  • Click on the pictogram featuring the letters A and Z to go directly to the index of the current manual.

  • Click on the pictogram featuring a house to get a description of the documentation delivered with this version of the product, and technical support information.

  • Click on the question mark to return to this page.

  • Click on the pictogram featuring a magnifying glass to launch a search of the documentation.

  • In each manual, you can resize the Table of Contents by clicking on its frame border. Click on the plus/minus sign to expand and contract the sub-menu displays.

  • A floating navigation bar on each page contains three arrows that allow you to go to the next page, the previous page, or back to to top of the current page.
